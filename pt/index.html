About This Book

In this book, children become true logical detectives, learning to identify and avoid the reasoning errors that we all face in our daily lives. With simple lessons, this guide teaches in an accessible and fun way what logical fallacies are and how to recognize them. Each chapter is an opportunity to explore everyday situations where thinking can play tricks. Through practical examples, children will learn to question, reason clearly and solve problems brilliantly, like true logical geniuses. This is the perfect book for those who want to develop critical skills from an early age, while having fun in an exciting intellectual challenge. Ideal for curious readers and for parents and teachers who want to encourage clear and creative thinking in children.
